Leverage Edu is one of the world's leading/fastest-growing ed-tech companies. It is transforming access to higher education and accelerating exponential career growth, by democratising mentorship, breaking traditional systems, and helping the new student of today achieve their dreams.

Students starting grade 8 onward can engage with global business and social leaders as part of Leverage Edu's acclaimed Mentorship product, which helps them discover their unique strengths through career workshops, assessments and online plus offline activities.

Design
Challenges

01

Content Discoverability is one issue that leverage as a platform has always struggled with. In Spite of having all the key information that is valuable to their users on their website, they would often get enquiries on the phone from students asking for the same information.

02

As a platform that promised students to get closer to their career goals, Leverage Edu had to gain the trust of its users. Since the number of competitors in this space is particularly high, it was important to appear as a company that is trustworthy and an expert in this space.

03

We also needed to design an SEO friendly interface to increase user traction and retention.

User
Research

Heatmap analysis on the old website helped us understand the crucial user journey on the Leverage platform; we had a better understanding of the user's search history, engagements, and drop off points. We also interviewed some of the existing users on the platform to map out the three unique user personas we would be catering to, viz, school students, college students, and working professionals. Here’s what we discovered.

A Design led by Personas

3 different personas, 3 different landing pages.

The platform has three widely varied personas to cater to, we decided to prioritise the college student user persona as the primary one and redesigned the content on the first fold to cater to them. Since the content for each target audience was vastly different, we decided that it is impossible to have all of them under one fold. Hence, the separate landing pages were designed for each target audience and content specific to their interest was populated there

Building trust with every scroll.

The primary goal of every section on the home page was to increase the trustability of the brand. The content was designed to ensure that the users perceive Leverage Edu to be the experts in this field. Students' testimonials, awards and recognition, renowned mentors, and the timeline of the brand were highlighted for gaining user's trust

Providing context before asking users to engage.

We also improved the user flow for all the sections by building context and providing statistics on how some services offered by leverage can help the user thereafter asking people to click on the CTA to avail some free services.

Tools to drive engagement

‘Find you best fit university’ is a tool where Leverage Edu's Proprietary AI Algorithm matches your profile and credentials against millions of data points and finds your 'Perfect University Fit' in less than 2 minutes. The user has to input if he/she is a student in school, student in university or a working professional. Post which, the tool asks for some exam scores, last education and work experience. Based on these data points, it generates a list of universities where the student would be a good fit. Since a lot of students who reach out to Leverage Edu do not know which universities they should apply to, this tool was used extensively by users.

Increasing ‘Time Spent’ by adding real value

Cost of Living Calculator is a tool that helps students get a ballpark monthly expense based on the location as well as the preferred lifestyle. The tool takes input from a student about his/her living situation, food habits etc to arrive at this figure. Since cost is always a concern for students, this helps students make an informed decision.

The Visual Language

We designed a unique icon for every user persona which repeats all over the platform. This helps the user identify content catered for them easily. We used a simple sans serif font “Montserrat” which comes in various font weights that helped us achieve the visual hierarchy we wanted. Since the website was quite text heavy, we used a lot of detailed illustrations that gave the eye a break from all the text. Each illustration was designed specific to the content in that section and followed a flat style. Apart from using grayscale for the text, we used pastel colors for the illustrations to increase the contrast between the text and the visuals.
