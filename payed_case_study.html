Payed

Simplifying education loans for parents of all backgrounds

Client

Payed

Platform

Android and iOS Application

Deliverables

UX Research, UX & UI Design

Payed asked a simple question - with education so expensive, what can we do to make it easy for parents to acquire and repay the loans they take for the betterment of their children?

Their offerings cater to the child from KG to PG with a zero collateral and zero deposit. For parents, the loan is a boon, but the interest is what concerns them. Banks in India usually take 1-3% of interest on loans and the repayment period is between 5-7 years.

Design
Challenges

01

to formulate a website and web dashboard that facilitates easy education loans repayments, and tracking for parents

02

augment emotions and convey vital principles in a gratifying manner about education loans.

03

a consistent design system for their new product to enhance their digital presence and brand.

04

to position Payed as friendly, supportive, and caring for the needs of children and their education.

User
Research

To understand the parents better, we drew out user personas based on factors we felt were important to how decisions are made.

Illustrations
that ignite emotions

The first interaction a parent has with Payed must elicit a sentimental response; with this thought in mind, we incorporated soft illustrations that echoed emotions of support, responsibility, and care. The three characteristics of our tone of voice and language were: respectful, formal and enthusiastic. The text should speak to the reader in the second person, like a friend who's passing good advice. Throughout the website, we formed a conversational relationship with the user, where they feel that Payed supports them, understands them, and is easy to use.

Onboarding, the UX Flow

We broke the process up into two digestible segments - the basic information gathering, and a soft credit check. This was arranged in a way so as to not overwhelm the parent. Throughout the process, we introduced pre-action cues such as 'please keep your passport ready' to inform and guide pleasantly.

Beneficiary Dashboard

Having a visual appeal reminiscent of sticky notes, the parent dashboard was created with the ideology the user should not be overwhelmed with numbers and text, given that the numbers may be in large sums, and too much text can be frustrating.

Fast, easy, simple payments

Payed could auto-import all payment details needed for the majority of schools and colleges in India, and combine all the educational loan and fees payments into one simple monthly installment for you. All you needed to do was pay back Payed - they took care of everything else.

Communication Emailers

The email templates that we created for Payed were of two kinds - transactional and promotional. The promotional emails were initiated to urge parents to complete the process as Payed benefits them, and the transactional emails were utilised as a touch point to confirm actions taken.

"If we showed our product to 100 users, 90 of them would say it's accessible and easy to follow."

NV Subramaniam,
 Co-Founder and CEO at Payed
